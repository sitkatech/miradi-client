Help with Miradi Software

To get answers to questions or problems, we recommend the following steps:

  1. Answers to many "how-to" questions can be found in the Step-by-Step instructions in the green panels at the top of the screen. You can use the <Step-By-Step> menu or the <Instructions> button found at the bottom of many screens to jump to the relevant spot in the interview sequence. You may also want to consult the list of Frequently Asked Questions at: miradi.org/help.

  2. For content oriented questions, you might also want to consult the blue <More Info> button at the top of each view.

  3. If you have obtained a license from the Miradi.org website, you can also e-mail support questions to: support@miradi.org.
    We will endeavor to answer your questions as soon as possible.

  4. Finally, if you have obtained a license from a CMP Partner, you can contact the e-mail address for your organization as shown below.
    • Foundations of Success (FOS) - fos@miradi.org
    • Rare Conservation - rare@miradi.org
    • The Nature Conservancy (TNC) - tnc@miradi.org
    • Wildlife Conservation Society - wcs@miradi.org
    • WWF - wwf@miradi.org

Intro to Schedule View - Demo

This view will enable you to develop schedules for implementing your project actions and monitoring as outlined in your work plan. As shown by the screen shots on the tabs below, functions in this view will enable your team to:
  • Develop a project calendar showing activities/tasks and major events on a given day, week, or month either for specific team members, or for the project as a whole.
  • Create a Gantt chart showing how different activities/methods and tasks link to one another

Note that Miradi will not recreate the full functionality of sophisticated project management programs such as Microsoft Project. Miradi will, however, enable you to undertake essential functions like:

  • Have Miradi issue pop-up alerts when specific tasks need to be completed
  • Plan sequences of tasks working either forwards or backwards in time
  • Assess when specific team members are either over or underscheduled
  • Enable team members to record their time spent on various activities and then generate timesheets and reports
  • Maintain a chronological history of project work and milestones

The first step in building a conceptual model is to put your project scope to one side (usually the far right) of your workspace.

To insert your scope, click on the <Insert Scope Box> button in the Control Bar. Miradi will add a project scope box to your diagram. You can select and drag the scope box to wherever you want it to be. You can then double-click on the green Project Scope box to either review the scope you entered earlier or enter a new scope statement.

New Feature Hint: You can also have more than one scope box to depict more complex project situations. The scope box is not a Group Box - if you want to link to all items in your Scope Box you either must insert a Group Box into the Scope Box or link to all them individually.

Many threats affect more than one target. To link your threat to another target, select the direct threat, hold the <Shift> key and then select the desired target. Then click on the <Insert Link> button (the arrow). You can also link direct threats to each other using the <Insert Link> button (the arrow).

Hint: The best way to link two factors in Miradi is to select the first one, hold the shift key, select the second one, and then click the <Insert Link> button (the arrow) in the Control Bar. This method avoids the need to scroll through a lengthy list of all factors in the system.

Hint: If you end up with spaghetti - many crossing links - you can uncheck "target links" on the Control Bar to hide these links except when you click on a factor.

Hint: If you are using stresses, you can position your stress boxes on a link between the threat and the target to display your stresses in the diagram.

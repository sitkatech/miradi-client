Welcome to the Work Plan View

A work plan shows the specific activities / methods and tasks that you need to undertake to implement your strategic and monitoring plans.

The work plan view also enables you to develop a budget for your project (in the future, it will also enable you to manage actual expenditures). The budget shows the planned costs and funding sources for the activities/methods and tasks that you need to undertake to implement your strategic and monitoring plans.

As a general rule, you should have a detailed work plan for the coming year and a rough plan for the years after that.

New Feature Hint:In Miradi 3.0, the former Planning View has been split into the Strategic and Work Plan views and has been dramatically improved.

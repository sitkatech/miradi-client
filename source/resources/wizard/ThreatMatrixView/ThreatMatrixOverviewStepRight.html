Miradi supports two different threat rating systems:
  • Simple Threat Rating is a system developed by the Conservation Measures Partnership. It involves rating the impact of direct threats on targets.

  • Stress Based Threat Rating is a system developed by The Nature Conservancy. It involves rating the impact of stresses on a target and then the contribution of sources of stress (direct threats) on each stress.
Both methods meet the CMP Open Standards in that they each provide a prioritzed list of direct threats affecting each target. Click on the <More Info> button to learn more about the two methods.

Map View - Demo

This view will enable you to develop spatial maps of your project site. As shown by the screen shots on the tabs below, functions in this view will enable your team to:
  • Obtain a base map of your project site
  • Outline the scope of your project area
  • Show targets, threats, and other objects on the map as points or polygons

Note that Miradi will not recreate the full functionality of sophisticated Geographic Information Systems (GIS) programs.  It will, however, be able to import files produced using common formats.  Furthermore, Miradi will link information generated in other views to the map, potentially allowing you to do things like:

  • Conduct basic spatial analyses (union or intersection of polygons, etc)
  • Show how target or threat status varies over your project site
  • Depict the spatial "footprint" of a given strategy or objective
  • Prioritize strategies and actions or monitoring work in different sections of your project area
  • Upload project information to spatially referenced databases

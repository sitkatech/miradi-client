The next step is to develop viability ratings for each indicator.

For a selected indicator, type in the value range field for the "good" rating what you think the minimum acceptable range of variation might be for that indicator.  You can then also specify what might be in the "fair" value range. These ratings will then appear in the table at the top in the appropriate column.

Welcome to the Planning View

This view enables you to develop and edit various plans for your project. Standard planning views include:

Strategic Plan - A plan for how your project will change the project situation. Is primarily derived from results chains and shows the relationship between a project's goals, objectives, and strategies.

Monitoring Plan - A plan for collecting information that you and others need to know about your project. Is primarily derived from results chains and includes the indicators that you will track over time as well as the methods that you will use.

Work Plan - A description of the specific activities/methods and tasks that you need to undertake your strategic and monitoring plans. Shows the timing and sequence of each task as well as the specific resources required to carry it out. Most projects will only do a detailed work plan for the next 1-2 years of their project.

Goals Only, etc. - Shows all of your goals, objectives, targets, direct threats, strategies, activities, indicators, methods, or assigned resources in one table for quick comparisons and editing.

In addition to these standard planning views, Miradi also enables you to create your own custom planning views.

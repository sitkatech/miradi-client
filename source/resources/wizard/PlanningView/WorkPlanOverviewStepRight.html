Navigation Hints for the Work Plan Sub-View

The Work Plan Sub-View has an expanded interface from the basic Planning View. Click on the Work Plan radio button to see an overview of your plan. In the tree, clicking on the + and - symbols enables you to expand or collapse different parts of the hierarchy. Items in the table can include activities, methods, and tasks (note that tasks apply to both activities and methods). Click on the buttons below to add, delete, or move an item up and down the hierarchy so that they appear in the proper sequence.

The bottom panel is used to assign resources to each item. Assignments can be made at the level of an activity/method or its component tasks.

You can edit the name of activity or method directly or by clicking on the Activities Only or Methods Only radio buttons. Clicking on a row in the table will allow you to bring up and edit the information for that row. Click on the Resources tab to create, edit, or delete a resource.

At this point, you should have already created your draft strategic plan by assigning specific goals, objectives, and strategies to specific factors in the diagram view.

Click on the <Strategic Plan> radio button in the Planning View to view and edit this plan. You can look at your plans either based on your overall conceptual model, or on specific results chains by collapsing and expanding elements of the tree.

Note that in your strategic plan, a given strategy may be repeated several times if it contributes to more than one goal or objective. (In your work plan, by contrast, strategies are not repeated.) If you want to adjust which strategy or activity is tied to a given objective, click on the objective in the upper tree table. In the panel below, click on the <Choose Strategies And Activities> button and check which strategies and activities you would like to have associated with this objective.

For a selected strategy, you can also use the <Edit Progress Report> button to update the status of a given indicator.

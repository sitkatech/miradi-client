4A. Develop Detailed Work Plans

Develop Activities and Tasks

Each strategy will have on or more activities associated with it. On the <Work Plan> tab, select a strategy that you want to focus on. Think about the specific activities that you will have to undertake to carry out this strategy. Click on the <Create Activity> button to add a new activity and then type the name of the activity in the label box in the bottom panel. Repeat for other activities for this strategy as needed. You can use the <Move Item Up> and the <Move Item Down> buttons to change the order.

If necessary, you can also highlight an activity and click on the <Create Task> button to add tasks under this activity. (If you are really detail oriented, you can even create sub-tasks under tasks.)

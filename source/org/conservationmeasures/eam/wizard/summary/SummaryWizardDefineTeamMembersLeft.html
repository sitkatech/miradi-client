The first step in the project cycle involves specifying who will be on the initial project team.

To develop your team, click on the <Create Member> button and fill in the appropriate fields. Miradi treats team members as human resources for the project. Fields include:

  • Resource ID - The team members initials or an abbreviation for their position.
  • Resource - The name of the team member.
  • Roles - The roles that the person plays on the team.
  • Organization / Position / Phone / Email - Information about the team member.
  • Unit(s) / Cost Per Unit - Salary or other payment information about the team member.
    • Click the <Close> button when you are finished. Add other team members as necessary.

    Note that team members often include people from other organizations. In some cases, team members/human resources are not just one person, but rather a sub-team or class of indivdiuals - for example, a biological monitoring team.

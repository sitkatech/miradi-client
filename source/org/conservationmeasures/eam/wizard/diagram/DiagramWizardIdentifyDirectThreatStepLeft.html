To insert a direct threat, select a target and then click the <Insert Direct Threat> button located on the Legend Panel. The software will add a new direct threat linked to the target and open a dialog box.

Type the name of the direct threat. You can also add additional information about the threat in the Comment field. You should also pick the entry that best describes your threat from the standard classification of direct threats in the drop-down menu.

Using the mouse or arrow keys, move the threat to wherever you want it to be.

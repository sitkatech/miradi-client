Draft strategies can be rated on the following criteria:

  • Impact - Will the strategy have a high or low level of impact on the situation at your project site?

  • Duration - Will the strategy lead to permanent or long-term change or will it only have short-term impact?

  • Feasibility - Is the strategy easy or extremely difficult to carry out?

  • Cost - Is the strategy inexpensive to carry out? Or is it prohibitively expensive?

These ratings are then rolled-up to give an overall summary rating for the strategy. Note that if a draft strategy is assigned any "red" colored rating, then the summary rating will also be "red."

Once you have developed your draft strategies, you rate each one in terms of its potential impact and feasibility by double clicking on a draft strategy and using the drop-down ratings in the dialog box.

Once you have developed all your draft strategies, you can decide which one(s) you want to carry forward. Unchecking <draft> in the status field in the dialogue box will convert the strategy from a light yellow "draft strategy" to a dark yellow "strategy."

Click the <Show Full Diagram> button (available at the bottom of the screen, the toolbar ,or by right-clicking the mouse) to go back to the full model and hide all your draft strategies. Note that your draft strategies are still retained in <Brainstorm Mode>.

You can also view and edit all your project's strategies using the Strategies Only setting in the Planning View.
